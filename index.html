Campus life

KULACHI HANSRAJ MODEL SCHOOL

Kulachi Hansraj Model School, India, was established in 1972 by the late Shri Darbari Lal. It is a Dayanand Anglo Vedic (D.A.V.) school which educates students from kindergarten to senior secondary school grades. The school began in the 1970s with 60 students and four teachers in two classrooms. The school's motto is "to strive, to seek, to find and not to yield". It is an ISO 9001:2000 certified school. Kulachi Hansraj Model School has three wings across Ashok Vihar. The main and high school branch is in phase III, while the primary school and the nursery branch is in phase I. The school had 7,500 students on its roll in 2005, 300 teachers and a non-teaching staff of 150 in four campuses, under the guidance of Chairman Late Padamshree G. P. Chopra and Mrs. Sneh Verma.

Global Innovative School
KHMS has been declared winner of Global Innovative Schools Award 2018 held at BITS Pilani Dubai Campus on 27.1.2018

Global Millennial Conference was held on 27th January, 2018 at BITS Pilani Dubai Campus. 52 Schools from India and Dubai were awarded GISA 2018 (Global Innovative Schools Award) for their exemplary performance in Academics, Use of technology, Cultural Inclusion and Social Awareness. 16 schools from Delhi and NCR were in the list of winners. This award was first set of Global awards initiated by Phyzok in collaboration with BITS Pilani Dubai. Ms. Rashmi Kathuria, PGT(Mathematics) received the award on school’s behalf
